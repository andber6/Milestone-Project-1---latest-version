My life and how I went from bodybuilding to Software Development


About me


I am 20 years old and currently enrolled in Code Institute's Software Development course. I have never coded prior to this course, but my dad has been programming for as long as I can remember, so I guess it was just a matter of time before I decided to take a look at it myself. It's quite a new shift for me as I have been bodybuilding and into fitness for over half a decade, but I love learning new things and especially to challenge myself. I have actually competed in Men's Physique 3 times before and have included a couple of pictures from my last competition in August 2019.

I was born in Switzerland and lived there for 2 years before my family moved back to Norway, where I was raised (mostly). For my 6th grade year my family decided to move to Cape Town, South Africa. During my time here I went to American International School of Cape Town for a year before we went back home. After completing Norwegian highschool (10th grade), I felt like I wanted to do something with my life. Although I was a good kid and did what I was supposed to, I always felt like I wanted to become more productive and make something of my life. After being inspired by a military weekend I attended during 9th grade, I was looking everywhere online for opportunities for me. Eventually I came across Marine Military Academy in Harlingen, Texas, which I ended up attending for my junior year of highschool (11th grade).

I enjoy solving challenges and coming up with different solutions. I especially enjoy the feeling I get when I complete a challenge without having to look it up or research it. There are already numerous things and other courses that I already have decided on that I want to learn more about, but it'll have to wait until I finish Code Institute's Software Development program first.

My experience


The languages I have gotten to experience with this far are HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. I have also been utilizing some bootstrap.

I have worked in a quite a wide variety of jobs, ranging from fieldmarketing to finance as well as a gym and did not see myself ending up as a software developer until I attended Code Institute's Software Development seminar here in Oslo during the summer of 2019. However, even though I have not been learning software development for a very long time. I can say with confidence that I have learned a lot these past months, but most importantly, I have enjoyed the process and can definitely see myself as a future fullstack web developer or something similar in a few years.

My future plans include learning more about cyber security once I have a firm understanding of how Software Development works. I believe the foundation that I will build my knowledge on is very important to focus on first.
